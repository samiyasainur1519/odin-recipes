Pizza

Even a novice cook can master the art of perfect pizza with our step-by-step guide.

This homemade pizza recipe features a simple pizza dough and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 300g bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 100ml passata
  • handful of basil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 125g ball mozzarella
  • handful of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the flour into a large bowl, then stir in the yeast and salt. Make a well, pour in 200ml warm water and the olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft, fairly wet dough. Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5 mins until smooth.
  2. Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste.
  3. Give the flours a quick knead, then split into two balls. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  4. Heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season. Put a pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp.
